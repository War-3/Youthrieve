Molly White Tracks Crypto Scams. It’s Going Just Great

The software engineer’s cautionary Web3 blog pours cold water on cryptocurrency’s dumpster fires.

White started the blog in December 2021 out of frustration with the mainstream coverage of crypto, which she says paid too much attention to rags-to-riches tales and not enough to its dark underbelly.

A software engineer by trade, White coded Web3 Is Going Just Great over the span of a few weeks.
