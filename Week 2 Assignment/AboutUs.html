About the Company

from banks

Vetsark is a technology company that helps farmers and agribusinesses in Africa
access finance, increase productivity, and improve their profitability. We use
technology to bridge the gap between farmers and financial institutions by providing
a platform for loan origination, credit scoring, and loan monitoring. More importantly,
we help farmers source inputs and sell their products at fair prices. Our goal is to unlock
the potential of African agriculture by providing farmers with the financing and support
they need to grow their businesses. We believe that agriculture is the key to unlocking
Africa's $1 trillion economic potential (AfDB), and we are committed to playing our part
in making that a reality.








Our Mission

Build with our partners in agriculture the opportunity to
manage & finance their agribusinesses, using the right tech
tools, guiding them to best performance and increased
profitability for a sustainable growth

Our Vision

Transform agricultural financing in Africa




Our Aspiration

At Vetsark, our aspiration is not to have the most used software or fintech. We are in the business
of helping farmers prosper. We are reinventing the fundraising experience for farms and
agribusinesses, and taking the hard work out of the fundraising process.
